## Key Observations

1. **Western/European names dominate** the ADGM FSRA register at 25.4%, 
   reflecting ADGM's position as an international financial centre staffed heavily by expat finance professionals.

2. **Arab/MENA names** represent 23.7% of all individuals, reflecting 
   local and regional representation in Abu Dhabi's financial sector.

3. **South Asian names** at 8.9% reflect the significant South Asian 
   professional community in UAE financial services.

4. **East/Southeast Asian names** at 3.3% represent professionals 
   from Singapore, Hong Kong, mainland China, Korea, and Japan.

5. **1038** individuals (38.7%) could not be 
   classified due to ambiguous or uncommon name patterns.

6. The most common first name is **David** (31 occurrences), 
   and the most common surname is **Khan** (19 occurrences).
