Cross-UAE comparison

ADGM FSRA (Abu Dhabi)

20+ firms licensed for VA activities. English common law. Institutional focus. Covers broker-dealers, custodians, exchanges, asset managers, FRT issuers. 2,681 approved individuals on the public FSRA register (classified + nationality-coded in research data).

DIFC DFSA

January 2026 major crypto framework update. Covers recognised crypto tokens, investment tokens, trading, custody, asset management. Siloed from Dubai mainland — DFSA covers DIFC only. Law No. 4 of 2022 Article 3 excludes DIFC from VARA jurisdiction.

Total UAE licensed VA firms: estimated 85+ (VARA 45 + ADGM 20+ + DIFC growing). World's most comprehensively regulated VA jurisdiction.

Glossary · Regulatory and technical vocabulary

VARAVirtual Assets Regulatory Authority — Dubai's dedicated crypto regulator, established under Law No. 4 of 2022.
VASPVirtual Asset Service Provider — any entity performing regulated VA activities under VARA.
IDQInitial Disclosure Questionnaire — first form submitted to VARA; triggers preliminary assessment.
ATIApplication & Technical Information — second stage after IDQ clearance. Full licence application.
IPAIn-Principle Approval — conditional licence. Firm cannot serve clients until Full Market Product licence is granted.
FMPFull Market Product licence — permits live operations and client servicing.
Cat 1 VA IssuanceCategory 1 Virtual Asset Issuance licence — permits issuing new tokens. Only 2 entities hold it.
ARVAAsset-Referenced Virtual Asset — token backed by one or more physical or financial assets (e.g. real estate).
FRVA / FRTFiat-Referenced Virtual Asset / Token — stablecoin pegged to a fiat currency. Often requires CBUAE oversight.
BDBroker-Dealer — facilitates buying/selling of VA for clients. Principal (own book) or agent (arranges trades).
EXExchange Services — operates a multilateral, automated matching engine for VA trades.
M&IManagement & Investment Services — manages VA portfolios or funds.
CUCustody Services — safekeeping of client Virtual Assets (includes custodial staking variant).
MLROMoney Laundering Reporting Officer — named individual on VARA application responsible for AML reporting.
KYC / CDDKnow Your Customer / Customer Due Diligence — onboarding checks required under AML regime.
SPVSpecial Purpose Vehicle — bankruptcy-remote entity holding one specific asset. Common in tokenization structures.
ERC-3643 / T-REXTokeny's permissioned security token standard on Ethereum. Compliance-enforced at smart-contract level.
DMCCDubai Multi Commodities Centre — free zone commonly used for VASP incorporation.
DIFC / DFSADubai International Financial Centre / Dubai Financial Services Authority — separate Dubai free zone + regulator. Siloed from VARA.
ADGM / FSRAAbu Dhabi Global Market / Financial Services Regulatory Authority — Abu Dhabi's free zone and VA regulator.
DLDDubai Land Department — Dubai's property registry. Issues certificates of ownership for tokenized real estate.
SCA → CMASecurities & Commodities Authority replaced by Capital Market Authority on 1 Jan 2026 under Federal Decree-Laws 32/2025 & 33/2025. CMA has extraterritorial reach over UAE-targeting persons.
CBUAECentral Bank of the UAE — regulates fiat + Payment Token Services (stablecoin issuance) across the federation.
Howey TestUS case law defining an "investment contract" (investment of money · common enterprise · expectation of profit · from efforts of others). Used by substance-over-form doctrine.
Substance over formRegulatory doctrine: economic reality of a transaction beats its legal wrapper. Applied in Telegram, Terraform, Ripple cases.
